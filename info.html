🚌 Local Transit
Port Authority of Allegheny County

Pittsburgh's bus network connects most major neighborhoods. The 61 bus line runs frequently between Downtown, Oakland (CMU/Pitt area), and Shadyside.

App: Transit App or Google Maps for real-time tracking.

Fare: $2.75 per ride. Day passes available.
