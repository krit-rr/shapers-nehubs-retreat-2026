Bridges of Belonging

We, the Global Shapers of Pittsburgh, are so excited to welcome you to our home and to a weekend centered on exactly what this city embodies: connection across distances, resilience through reinvention, and the belief that the strongest structures are built together. Pittsburgh is a city literally built on bridges (more than any other city in the world), and so we found it fitting to use the retreat as an invitation to bridge the divides between hubs, ideas, and differences — and to leave with relationships and momentum that extend well beyond these three days.
